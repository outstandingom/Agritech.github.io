How to Map Your Land

  1. Click Start Tracking to begin location tracking
  2. Allow location access when prompted by your browser
  3. Move to each corner of your land and click Add Corner
  4. Add at least 3 corners to define your land boundary
  5. Click Finish Land to save the land
  6. Repeat for additional lands
